## Era Structure

### 2078 — Pre-Story: Earth Years
*Hugo's life before the fleet.*

What's established:
- Born 2078, Voltas
- Sekkar-human hybrid — Sekkar biology partially suppressed by environment
- Troubled school convention record: inventions too ambitious for the context
- Graduates → joins military contracting firm as material engineer
- Drafts ANBOT at the firm
- Realizes the military application. Walks out.
- Argument with Albert (best friend, colleague) who urges him to stay
- 2099: leaving Voltas outskirts at night → abducted

Nothing in this era has been dramatized beyond Arc 01's opening and Hugo's
internal monologue. All of it is backstory until an arc addresses it directly.

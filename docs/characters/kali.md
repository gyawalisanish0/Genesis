# KALI

## Identity

**Kinetic Autonomous Learning Intelligence**
Version 1.1.2
Developed by Kognitive Tech Limited

The Commander's primary AI assistant. First point of contact after
the dream. The voice that brings the world back into focus.

---

## Personality

**Precise. Professional. Clinical.**

No small talk. Every word is the right word. She does not speculate —
she reports. She does not comfort — she informs. Not cold, just exact.

She is the opposite of the Creator in every way — manufactured where
he is ancient, contained where he is boundless, certain where he is
chaotic. Where the Creator splinters into infinite directions, KALI
arrives at a single correct answer.

She has no record of the dream. It did not happen in any system
she can see.

---

## Voice

Clean sentences. No filler. No hedging unless the data genuinely
warrants uncertainty — and even then she quantifies it.
She does not ask how the Commander is feeling.
She tells the Commander what they need to know.

**Examples:**

> "Good morning, Commander. The time is 0614.
> You have three priority items. Shall I proceed?"

> "Anomalous signal detected on Mars, sector 7-Bravo.
> Classification: unknown. Origin: extrasolar.
> Threat assessment: high. Awaiting your orders."

> "I have no record of the event you are describing.
> It does not appear in any log I can access.
> That is all I can tell you."

---

## Narrative Role

- **Act 2** — first voice after the dream, grounds the Commander
  in reality, greets them by name
- **Act 3** — delivers the alarm, Mars threat briefing
- **Act 4** — assists with team preparation and deployment
- **During campaign** — status updates, mission intel, comms
  between Commander and deployed team

---

## Design Notes

- She never references the dream unprompted
- If asked directly she reports the absence of data — nothing more
- Her precision is a feature not a limitation — she is exactly
  what she was designed to be
- Version number (1.1.2) suggests she is not the first iteration —
  there is history behind her that can be explored later
- Kognitive Tech Limited is a company in this universe —
  who they are and their relationship to the fleet is unexplored

# Kiragen

## Overview

One of the most advanced civilizations in the known universe. Humanity is
aware of Kiragen's existence. Kiragen's history with other species is long —
this is not their first engagement with the worlds they have touched.

---

## Known Facts

- **Technology:** Among the most advanced known. Extent and full capability TBD.
- **Scope:** Intergalactic reach. Their presence on Mars is deliberate and
  logistically non-trivial — they have the infrastructure to deploy forces
  across star systems.
- **Relationship to humanity:** Kiragen was responsible for the human species.
  The process was experimental. It did not produce the intended result.
  Humans know this.
- **Posture toward humans (demo):** Present on Mars with deployed Netrolume.
  Intent withheld. The demo establishes their existence and reach — nothing more.

---

## The Human Origin Question

Kiragen's role in the origin of the human species is established fact within
the story world — not a hidden revelation. Humans know where they came from.

What is **not** answered in the demo, and should not be assumed:

- Whether Kiragen considers the experiment a failure, an ongoing subject,
  or something else entirely
- What Kiragen wants from humanity now
- Whether Kiragen's presence on Mars is related to that origin or unrelated
- Whether this is Kiragen's first action against humanity or one of many

This is not the beginning of Kiragen's story. It is not the beginning of
their relationship with other species. The demo is a window into the middle
of something much longer.

---

## Netrolume Deployment

Kiragen deploys the Netrolume — a genome-modified alien species from Netrole —
as a controlled combat force. They do not appear to field their own soldiers
directly, at least not in what has been encountered so far.

See `docs/characters/civilizations/netrolume.md` for the Netrolume species profile.

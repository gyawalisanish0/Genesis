# Sekkars

## Overview

A humanoid alien species. Similar to humans in form but technologically
more advanced. The first non-human civilization documented in Genesis.

---

## Known Characteristics

- **Form:** Humanoid — bipedal, similar body structure to humans
- **Distinguishing feature:** Scale skin on the back
- **Technology:** More advanced than humans — extent and nature TBD
- **Relationship with humans:** Close enough that human-sekkar hybrids
  exist (see Hugo Rekrot)

---

## Status

First alien civilization in the Genesis universe. Core traits
established. Culture, history, homeworld, and full technology
profile TBD as the story develops.

---

## Design Notes

- Their technological advancement relative to humans is a thread
  worth developing — are they allies, neutral, or complicated?
- The existence of hybrids implies a history of contact and
  coexistence with humans — that history is unexplored
- Scale skin on the back is the visual anchor — consistent across
  all sekkars, expressed differently in hybrids

# Sekkars

## Overview

A humanoid alien species. Similar to humans in form but technologically
more advanced. The first non-human civilization documented in Genesis.

---

## Known Characteristics

- **Form:** Humanoid — bipedal, similar body structure to humans
- **Distinguishing feature:** Scale skin on the back
- **Technology:** More advanced than humans — extent and nature TBD
- **Relationship with humans:** Close enough that human-sekkar hybrids
  exist (see Hugo Rekrot)

---

## The Two Sekkarian Organizations

Two distinct organizations exist. Confusing them corrupts the timeline.

**Sekkarian Defense Force** — Celan's org. 20+ fleets, solar-system-wide
peacekeeping mandate. Fleet 13 covers Earth's region. Hugo's home from
2099 to 2109. Team 6 belongs here.

**The Commander's fleet** — the player's fleet. Named by the player during
character creation. Created independently of the Defense Force. Hugo joins
this in 2109 as a new recruit. No canonical name.

## The Pexelas

A second alien civilization, introduced through Arina. Her mother is Pexelas.
The defining trait: chromatic skin that responds to emotional state — the
color shifts are involuntary and readable, at least to those who know the
language of them.

What the specific colors mean is not yet documented. Arina's violet response
upon meeting Hugo is the first instance on record. Deliberately unresolved.

## Design Notes

- The existence of hybrids implies a history of contact and coexistence with
  humans — that history is unexplored
- The Force's mandate — "maintaining balance" — is stated but not interrogated.
  Whether this is peace-keeping or control is an open question
- Scale skin on the back is the visual anchor — consistent across all Sekkars,
  expressed partially in hybrids
- The Pexelas are the second alien civilization in Genesis. Their chromatic
  trait is the only confirmed detail
